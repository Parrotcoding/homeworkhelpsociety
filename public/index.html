Fractions

A fraction represents a part of a whole. For example, 3/4 means 3 parts out of 4.

Tip: To add fractions, use a common denominator.

Decimals

Decimals are another way to represent parts of a whole. 0.5 is the same as 1/2.

Convert: To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator by the denominator.

Percentages
